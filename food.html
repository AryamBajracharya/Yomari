Yomari : Newari Dish

Yomari, also called yamari, is a delicacy of the Newar community in Nepal.It is a steamed dumpling consists of an external covering of rice flour with sweet fillings such as chaku.The delicacy plays a very important tole in Newa society, and is a key part of the festival of Yomari Punhi.


INGREDIENTS

  • 2 cups Rice flour
  • 1/3 All purpose flour
  • 2 cups Molasses sugar
  • 1tbsp Dry coconut(Shredded)
  • 1tbsp Brown sesame seed
  • 2tbsp Ghee
  • 1/2 Cup milk
  • 1/2 Cup water
